How to use:

Each craftable item has base stats, these materials enchance existing base stats. If an item doesn't have max health, you can't add max health the modifier will be ignored. The item will in most cases take on the colour of the first element in the crafting list, with accessories like buttons and buckles also taking on colours of the metal used.
